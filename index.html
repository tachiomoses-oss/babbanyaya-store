Our Story

Rooted in Culture,
Dressed for the World

BabbanYaya was born from a profound love of African artistry and a desire to bring its beauty to a global stage. Each piece we create is more than clothing — it is a story woven in thread, a dialogue between generations, a celebration of a continent whose creativity knows no bounds.

We partner directly with master artisans across Nigeria, Ghana, Senegal, and Côte d'Ivoire, ensuring fair wages, sustainable practices, and the preservation of centuries-old textile traditions. From the vibrant wax prints of Ankara to the regal stripes of Kente, every pattern carries meaning.
